Urban Ticket Design

Showcases bold type in a functional event format.

The Urban Beats ticket design is crafted in a wide rectangular format, reflecting the practical structure of real-world event tickets. Its use of clean sans-serif type ensures immediate readability, while a clear typographic hierarchy directs attention from event name, to time and location, then finally to barcode information.

The layout features bold color zones that divide the ticket into functional parts, such as entry validation and promotional space. The axial composition runs from left to right, guiding the eye naturally while reinforcing the ticket’s flow. The QR code placement supports modern scanning systems and the balance between white space and content reinforces a modern, breathable aesthetic.

Altogether, this typographic ticket presentation supports both aesthetic branding and real-use functionality, making it a powerful blend of design and purpose.

Through this typography project, I developed a deeper appreciation for how typefaces, layout, and composition influence the user experience. Each design allowed me to explore different structures, from strict modular grids to expressive axial arrangements.

This process not only improved my technical design skills in tools like Illustrator and Photoshop, but also gave me clarity on using typography to communicate mood, clarity, and brand identity effectively.
