The Art of CARP

Contrast, Alignment, Repetition, Proximity in Visual Design

Contrast

We used soft pink text on a dark mint background for the event title, helping it stand out and set the mood for Urban Beats.

Alignment

All elements were arranged in a symmetrical grid, making everything feel balanced and polished.

Repetition

Repeated circle shapes and typefaces gave the poster a clean, consistent identity across all materials.

Proximity

Text blocks like date, time, and venue were grouped together to help users quickly find relevant details.

Poster Redesign Before & After

Before (Bad)

Cluttered layout, low contrast, too many fonts

After (Good)

Clean hierarchy, pastel tones, cohesive structure

This project was a creative challenge and a joyful learning experience. Designing for a music event like Urban Beats allowed me to explore playful color palettes and experiment with layout techniques.

Learning about CARP principles helped me think more critically about every design choice—from font pairing to element spacing. I now feel more confident using structure and contrast to guide the viewer’s eye and create designs that not only look good, but communicate effectively.
